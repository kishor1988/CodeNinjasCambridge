Unlock Your Child’s Potential Through Coding

At CodeNinjas Cambridge, we turn screen time into skill time. Children aged 5–14 learn to code by building video games, creating apps, and even programming robots — all while having a blast with friends in a safe, inspiring dojo environment.

Every child learns at their own pace, guided by our friendly Code Senseis. With a curriculum designed like martial arts, kids progress through coding “belts,” growing in confidence, creativity, and problem-solving ability as they level up.

Parents love the flexibility, and kids love the fun. It’s the perfect blend of play, learning, and future-ready skills.
